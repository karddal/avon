
# What happened to the agendas?

We initially began to take notes from our meetings in TB1. However, as time passed we stopped taking notes and added tasks on GitHub. This was not the best thing to do as we would fall behind on tasks.

Having realised from that experience, we decided to take notes as part of our weekly sprints. They can be accessed from the Sprints sections.
